Research

  • Sub-Neptunes

    Sub-Neptunes are the most abundant class of planet detected, despite the lack of one in our own solar system.

  • Exoplanet Interiors

    I use internal structure modelling to investigate the interiors and surfaces of sub-Neptune planets.

  • Habitability

    Hycean worlds are a class of sub-Neptune with hydrogen-rich atmospheres and water oceans, and have the potential to host habitable conditions.
